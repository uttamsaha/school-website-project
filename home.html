Welcome to Rajshahi Collegiate School

Rajshahi Collegiate School is the first and oldest traditional educational institution in the country. This school has a history of its own existence with the potential seeds of potential talent. Aishwarya has been cherishing the tradition for more than two and a half centuries. Lord William Benting, with his personal efforts and enthusiasm for the expansion of English education in the country, established the first school in 1826 under the name "Boulia English School". Its first activity began on a tiled verandah in a thatched dochala house near Barakuthi on the banks of the river Padmanabha. At first it was an unpaid private school.
